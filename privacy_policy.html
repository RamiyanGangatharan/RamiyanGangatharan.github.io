Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 27, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Harmony Hub. Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information. We are fully compliant with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and other relevant laws.

Collection of Personal Information

We gather personal information in the following ways:

  • When you register for an account on our website.
  • If you participate in our community activities and events.
  • When you reach out to us for support or inquiries.

Use of Personal Information

We use the information collected for several purposes:

  • To provide a personalized experience on our site.
  • To continually improve our website offerings and services.
  • To communicate with you effectively, particularly in response to your questions and feedback.

Sharing of Personal Information

Your privacy is critical to us. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may, however, share non-personally identifiable information with partners or advertisers purely for analytics and targeted advertising purposes.

Security of Personal Information

We take stringent security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Despite this, the inherent nature of the internet means that absolute security cannot be guaranteed, and we advise vigilance on all online activities.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this policy to reflect new practices or regulatory changes. We will post any changes on this page and encourage you to review our policy occasionally to stay informed.
